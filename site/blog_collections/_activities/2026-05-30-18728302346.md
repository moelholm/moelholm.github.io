More importantly😂: 🥉Age Group (3 of 10)

What an amazing experience 🤩. Many of Denmarks finest ultra runners ran along us mortals. It was incredible to watch their epic endurance carry on as my own gave into the pain cave 😆. And it was also extremely rewarding to observe how everyone out there gave it their absolute best on various individual goals: everybody fought soooo *hard*. 

Ultimately I am very satisfied with my performance: setting a personal PB wrt distance (prev: ~173K) and also just for the first time ever being on such a runnable course for 24 hours. 

As my muscles broke down I decided to make an experiment: instead of “keep on walk jogging” I  decided to gamble and take a 60 min full stop (read: sleep 😴 in my tent ⛺️). And … 🤯 … study the split times and it is evident what effect it had 😂👌🏻. So that got me reset and I could actually run again. Later on I repeated that trick but only for 30 minutes (to experiment further): same result 🤠🤗. So what could have been a death march actually ended up really well: almost 194 kilometers  🥳. And as a side effect this performance even placed me on the podium in my age group. That would not have happened if it wasn’t for my rest experiments. 

The volunteers were amazing. Their absolute positive and fun energy made a world of difference to us exhausted runners. Thank you volunteers - you made our day (and night … and next day 😂🥳🙏🏻). And the race organization was perfect too: everything clicked. 

In short: a perfect experience on all accounts 🤠🙌🏻
