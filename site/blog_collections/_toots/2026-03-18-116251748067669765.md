16K Run with another marathon tempo session inside 🚀😂

Today included a Runna tempo session. Over and unders. Like this: 

Run 4 x (1K fast + 1K faster). 

So 8K coherent higher pace. 

The trick is that the fastest kilometer overloads the blood with lactate. Then the other fast - but slower - kilometer tries to have you reuse the lactate (while under pressure still). This should “teach” you to become more efficient at clearing the lactate - before 🔥💥😵
